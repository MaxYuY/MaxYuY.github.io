Latin America

Latin America is home to some of the most extensive rainforests in the world as well as gorgeous tropical beach paradises. With places such as the jungles of Costa Rica's La Fortuna, Puerto Rico's El Yunque, and the Amazon Rainforest in Brazil, as well as the vast beaches of Central America and the Carribean islands, including Isla Culebra in Puerto Rico and Costa Rica's Manuel Antonio National Park, and much more, Latin America is full of cool places to explore. While I only had the chance to visit Costa Rica and Puerto Rico so far, the extensive natural beauty of Latin America ensures that I'll come back and visit more of this region.

Latin America is one of the most biodiverse regions in the entire world, and in my (limited) travels to both Puerto Rico and Costa Rica, I still seen plenty of amazing and unique wildlife. The Tapirs and Anteaters of Corcovado National Park, the sloths of Manuel Antonio National Park, and Sea Turtles of Isla Culebra are just some of the highlights during my trip in this amazing region.
