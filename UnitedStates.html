United States

The country I live in is probably one of the most beautiful in the world in terms of landscapes. From the deserts and rocky mountains in the west to the temperate forests and wetlands in the east, the United States has every type of landscape available and tons of things to see and do. The world famous canyons like Arizona's Grand Canyon and Utah's Zion Canyon, the breathtaking alpine vistas of Mt. Evans Scenic Byway in Colorado and Montana's Glacier National Park, dreamy coastline paradises like California's Monterey Bay, and crystal blue lakes like Lake Tahoe just some of the huge variety of places you'll find here and you're sure not to run out of thrilling and surreal experiences in the United States.

Within the stunning natural landscapes of the U.S resides some of the most iconic wildlife species in the world, as well as some of the most eye catching and unique animals to have ever stepped foot on this earth. From large mammals such as bears, moose, mountain goats, and bison to smaller creatures like ground squirrels, marmots, and rabbits, to aquatic animals like whales, seals, and sea otters, not to mention the massive amounts of birds that can be found here, the United States is a widlife haven, and visiting places like Yellowstone or Rocky Mountain National Parks will guarantee you with countless memorable wild animal encounters.
