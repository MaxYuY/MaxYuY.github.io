Monument Valley

Arizona's Monument Valley is iconic, home to some peculiar desert formations like Buttes, Mesas, and spires, all painting a beautiful and stunning picture of the valley that is certainly worth visiting.

The main attraction of Monument Valley has to be the scenic drive, taking you past a variety of different desert viewpoints and tons of magical scenery that simply looks awesome from up close. Thus, Monument Valley is certainly worth going out of the way to visit, especially considering how surreal it is to be standing within such gorgeous and otherworldly landscapes.
