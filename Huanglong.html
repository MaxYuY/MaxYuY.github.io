Huanglong

Located near Jiuzhaigou in Northern Sichuan, the Huanglong Scenic Area is just as beautiful as Jiuzhaigou and perhaps even more otherworldly. Huanglong is often paired with a visit with Jiuzhaigou, and the landform is formed by calcite deposits, creating these crystal clear turquoise pools on top of the golden landscape, which is just so awesome to see up close.

The hiking around the gorgeous pools of Huanglong is simply awesome. There are so many crazy views of the golden landform and the small turquoise lakes from giant cluster of lakes at the start to the glorious waterfalls at the end. Combined with the mountains in the backdrop, it just makes for the ultimate out-of-this-world experience in perhaps China's most underrated natural spot.
