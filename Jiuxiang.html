Jiuxiang

The Jiuxiang Scenic area near Kunming in the Yunnan province is home to some of the most amazing caves and karst scenery in China. The park has the whole package: Besides the massive caves, I was treated to plenty of cool river and canyon-like scenery making it so worth visiting.

On my visit, to get to the caves, I had to take two boat rides down the scenic Maitian River followed by a short hike, which for both I was surrounded by some amazing karst and river scenery in itself. The caves were obviously the highlight, with tons of unique and grand formations and even a waterfall inside, making Jiuxiang an place and experience that I'll never forget and one of the most underrated and uncrowded natural wonders I've been to in China.
