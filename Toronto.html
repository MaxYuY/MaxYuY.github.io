Toronto

Located in the Ontario Province, Toronto is the beating heart of Canada and one of the most beautiful cities in the entire world. Centered around the 553.3 meters tall CN Tower, the city has so many cool things to see and do. Its skyline is one of the most iconic and one of the stunning in the world as well, with many skyscrapers rising high up from the shores of Lake Ontario.

By far the best place to visit in the city and the main place I visited was the city's downtown harbour. The area around Toronto Harbour is stunning, as you are surrounded by a sea of skyscrapers towering above, making for a nice stroll around this beautiful city.

The wildlife by the Toronto Harbour was amazing. There were countless small ducks when we visited as well as a couple beautiful Mute Swans near the piers. The highlight though, had to be seeing a Red-Tailed Hawk on the pier of the harbour. The hawk was grabbing and feeding on a crow in its sharp talons, which was really cool to watch up close. In my opinion, Toronto has the perfect mix of nature and bustling city, certainly a top-tier city to me.
