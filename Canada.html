Canada

Known as the "Great White North", Canada is one of the most beautiful countries in the world. Cities like Vancouver and Toronto are clean and full of things to do, while Vancouver Island is one of the most beautiful places in the entire country, full of cool things to do and views to marvel at.

Canada's wildlife is so abundant. Both Vancouver and Toronto have great spots to see local birdlife, while Vancouver Island is one of the best places in Canada to search for more exotic wild animals, with Whales, Bears, Sea Otters, Seals, and so much more being found across this beautiful island and country.
