Haleakala National Park

Haleakala National Park in Maui is one of the most otherworldly places I've ever been to, centered around the namesake volcano that's over 3000 meters above sea level. The scenery at the top of Haleakala is simply breathtaking and makes the park one of my favorite places on Maui.

What makes Haleakala so unique and out-of-this-world is the Haleakala Crater accessed via the Sliding Sands Trail (top row), a brilliant volcanic landscape that's the closest thing you can get to Mars here on Earth. Within the barren but stunning scenery, I got to see a whole flock of the rare and beautiful Hawaiian Geese (bottom row) near the summit of Haleakala, making the gorgeous park and its views even more amazing.
