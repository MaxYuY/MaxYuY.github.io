Oceania and Pacific

Oceania and the Pacific Islands are literally paradise, full of stunning beach getaways and otherworldly landscapes. The volcanoes of Haleakala National Park in Maui and the colorful Waimea Canyon are just a few examples. Plus the Reefs of Maui and Reefs of Kauai are perfect for snorkeling, allowing you to admire the gorgeous and vast underwater world. While I haven't been to anywhere outside of Hawaii in Oceania, with so much to do in Hawaii, you can imagine the plethora of great sights and places to visit across this breathtaking and diverse region and I'll certainly go back to explore more.

The wildlife of Hawaii and Oceania in general is just as diverse as its landscapes and seascapes. On land, seeing the endangered Nenes or Hawaiian Geese in Haleakala National Park and Monk Seals on the beaches near Kauai's Reefs can be an unforgettable wildlife experience. Just as impressive are the huge number of Sea Turtles that can be found off the coast of Maui as well as dolphins on a Napali Coast tour in Kauai. If you visit Hawaii or any of the Pacific Islands in Oceania, you are sure to find some incredible wildlife on your visit.
