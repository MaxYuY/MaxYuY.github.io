Lake Tahoe

Located on the California-Nevada border, Lake Tahoe is one of the most famous lakes in the Western United States, with plenty of opportunities for adventure within the breathtaking views of the Sierra Nevada mountains.

During winter, skiing at Lake Tahoe is basically a must, with my favorite resort being Heavenly in both Nevada and California, which is by far the most scenic resort I've ever went to. The resort is unique, with views of Lake Tahoe on one side and the barren Nevadan desert on the other, and the sheer contrast between the two vastly different landscape was just awesome to see from Heavenly.

Emerald Bay state park on the shores of Lake Tahoe on the California side is absolutely beautiful, with beautiful viewpoints of the bay as well as great hiking such as the Eagle Falls Trail. Seeing the giant lake with snowy mountains in the background is always surreal and this state park is certainly a must-visit when you're in Lake Tahoe.

The Nevada side of Lake Tahoe is equally beautiful, with the highlights including Sand Harbor State Park and Memorial Point. Just like Emerald Bay, the crystal blue waters of the lake in front of the snowy Sierra Nevada mountains in both places are so cool in my opinion and another stunning destination if you're ever at Lake Tahoe.
