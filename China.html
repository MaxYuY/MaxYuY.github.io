China

Just like the U.S, the country of my ancestors is also incredibly diverse when it comes to landscapes. With deserts and mountains to the west, grassland and basins in middle, and vast open plains in the east, there's so much to see and do in China, with the alpine valleys of Jiuzhaigou and Huanglong National Parks in Sichuan, the Jiuxiang Caves and Jade Dragon Snow Mountain in Yunnan, and the Summer Palace and Great Wall of China in Beijing are just a few examples of the wide variety of places to visit in this breathtaking country.

My Favorite Places

Summer Palace

Stunning cultural site in the heartland of China's bustling capital

Jiuzhaigou

Beautiful alpine valley consisting of spectacular mountains and crystal blue lakes

Jade Dragon Snow Mountain

Nestled over 3000m high home to snow-capped peaks and gorgeous valleys

Most Underrated Places

Huanglong

Often paired with a visit to Jiuzhaigou, this lesser known valley has equally wonderful scenery

Jiuxiang Caves

Yunnan's otherworldly underground karst caves coupled with gorgeous river and gorge scenery
