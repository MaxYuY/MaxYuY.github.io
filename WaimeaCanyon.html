Waimea Canyon

Waimea Canyon on the island of Kauai is simply beautiful, and with its colorful scenery and its gorgeous views, it's no wonder why it's called the Grand Canyon of the Pacific and one of the best places to visit on the entire island and all of Hawaii.

The most popular and my favorite thing I did at Waimea Canyon was just simply admiring the canyon from the overlooks. Viewpoints like the main Waimea Canyon and Pu'u Ka Pele Lookouts bring stunning views of the canyon as well as the Waipo'o Falls in the distance: simply magical.

Besides the views of the gorgeous canyon, the other cool place at the Waimea Canyon State Park is the Red Dirt Waterfall area, which looks otherworldly up close, especially during the evening hours when the landscape turns into a fiery red, making the canyon scenery in this park even more magical to look at and admire.
