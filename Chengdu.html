Chengdu

The city of Chengdu is one of my favorite cities in the world, and the symbol of the city in Sichuan is one of my favorite animals, the Panda. Thus, seeing the Pandas are by far the highlights of the city and makes Chengdu the Panda capital of the world.

There are 4 Panda bases around Chengdu, and the biggest one is located in the center of the city. This base in downtown Chengdu is home to over 100 adorable pandas (top row), which I witnessed playing around and eating, one of the cutest things I've ever seen. The base also houses other animals like Red Pandas (Bottom left, bottom center) as well as majestic Black swans (bottom right) that make the Panda base stand out even more and Chengdu a highlight city in China.
